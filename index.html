Welcome to Hack Club!

Wheaton Hack Club is our local chapter sponsored by the original group based in California. Hack Club is a global network designed for programming clubs like us. Hack Club is student run. In other words: NO teachers and lectures! Hack Club is intended to be a supportive community for all members! Throughout the year, we will be completing awesome projects, learning programming languages, advancing previously acquired skills, and competing at hackathons. There is more to Hack Club then just our club meetings. Experience is not necessary for success. There is always something new to learn!
